Water descaling systems, also known as TSM (Total Scale Management) systems, are designed to remove scale and other mineral buildup from water, which can cause damage to plumbing systems and appliances over time. TSM systems use a process called ion exchange to remove the minerals that cause hard water, such as calcium and magnesium ions. The TSM system typically consists of two tanks filled with resin beads that attract and capture the minerals in the water. As the water flows through the tanks, the resin beads trap the minerals and release sodium ions, which do not cause scale buildup. Once the resin beads become saturated with minerals, the system automatically regenerates by flushing them with a saltwater solution. This process replenishes the sodium ions and allows the TSM system to continue removing scale and other mineral buildup from the water. By installing a TSM system in your home or business, you can protect your plumbing and appliances, reduce energy costs, and enjoy the benefits of soft, clean water.
